🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Mathagram! Here's how to begin your learning journey:

  • Create an account — Go to Sign Up with your email. Choose a display name (3-13 characters, A-Z only) and pick your avatar color.
  • Browse courses — Visit the Courses page. Filter by Math, Science, Data Analysis, Logic, Language, or Others.
  • Start a lesson — Click any course, then tap a lesson on the learning path. Green = playable, gray = locked.
  • Earn XP — Complete lessons to earn XP. Score higher for more stars (up to 3).
  • Track progress — Visit your Profile to see XP, streak, courses completed, and your AI Brain Scan.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn XP?
Complete any lesson quiz. Score 60%+ to earn XP. A perfect score (100%) gives bonus XP. XP is tracked on your profile.
How do stars work?
Each lesson gives 0-3 stars based on your score: A grade = 3 stars, B grade = 2 stars, C grade = 1 star. D or F = 0 stars.
What is the AI Brain Scan?
Go to your Profile and tap "Scan Brain IQ." It analyzes your XP, streak, and courses to generate Focus, Memory, Problem Solving, Creativity scores and an estimated IQ. You can also open your camera for a face scan! Scan daily to track your IQ over time.
What are the memory warnings?
Your brain scan memory score has warnings: 0-5% = Memory Loss (purple), 6-15% = Start Learning (blue), 85-94% = Stop Learning (yellow), 95-98% = Stop Learning (orange), 99-100% = Memory Full (red). These remind you to balance learning and rest!
How do I change my display name or color?
Go to your Profile, edit your name (3-13 characters, A-Z only), pick a new color from the color picker, and tap "Save Changes."
What is the Listening Practice?
Some lessons have headphone exercises where Edam the Bear and Diego take turns reading questions aloud. You answer without seeing the text — great for training your ears!
What is Edam's Impossible Math?
It's a Baldi's Basics-style math game! Problems 1 & 2 are solvable, but Problem 3 is always a glitched unsolvable question. Edam gets angrier with each notebook. It's a fun challenge — how far can you survive?
How does the translate button work?
Click the globe icon in the navigation bar, pick a language from the dropdown (36 available), and the page opens in Google Translate in a new tab.
What are the strike rules?
Mathagram uses a 3-Strike System enforced by Edam the Bear. Strike 1 = Warning. Strike 2 = 1-week suspension. Strike 3 = Permanent ban. Violations include bullying, spam, inappropriate content, and cheating. See our Terms of Service for full rules.
Is Mathagram free?
Yes! Mathagram is completely free. All courses, lessons, games, and features are available to everyone at no cost.
