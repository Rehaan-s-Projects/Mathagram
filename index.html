About Mathagram.org

What is Mathagram?

Mathagram is a 100% free online learning platform built by Rehaan Rashid. Interactive courses in math, science, programming, languages, music, brain puzzles, and more — with AI-powered quizzes, voice characters, and a Duolingo-style learning path. Real interactive practice modes: piano-staff song-tracks with scrolling notation, an 8×8 MIDI drum pad, Minecraft command-block typing drills, a kitchen-themed cooking quiz, and CAPTCHA-tribute mini-games. No ads, no paywalls.
