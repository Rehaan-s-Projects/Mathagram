Install from Browser

You can also install Mathagram directly from your browser — works on Android, iPhone, and desktop!

  1. Open mathagram.org in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone)
  2. Tap the menu (3 dots on Chrome, share button on Safari)
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  4. Mathagram appears as an app on your home screen!
