Community Safety Rules

Please read and agree before using Learning Post.

This community is ONLY for learning.

Learning Post is NOT social media. It is NOT for chatting, making friends, sharing personal life, posting selfies, or anything unrelated to education. This is a study community — share what you're learning, ask questions, and help others learn. Nothing else.

  • ✅
    Be Respectful — Treat everyone with kindness. No bullying, harassment, or hate speech of any kind. Harassment = Strike 1 warning. Repeated harassment = Strike 2 (1-week suspension). Third time = permanent ban.
  • 🔐
    Protect Your Privacy — Never share personal information like your full name, address, phone number, school name, or passwords. Do not ask others for their personal information.
  • 📚
    Education ONLY — Posts MUST be about learning, courses, study tips, homework help, or educational questions. No personal stories, social chat, drama, gossip, dating, or anything unrelated to studying.
  • 🚫
    No Inappropriate Content — No profanity, violent content, sexual content, racial slurs, or anything unsuitable for all ages. Using the N-word or posting pornographic content = IMMEDIATE permanent ban. No warnings.
  • 📷
    Education-Only Images — Only upload screenshots of your work, notes, diagrams, math problems, or study materials. No selfies, pets, memes, personal photos, or non-educational images.
  • 📢
    No Spam or Advertising — Do not post links, promotions, or repetitive messages. Do not promote your social media, YouTube, Discord, or any other platform.
  • ⚖
    No Cheating — Do not share answers or quiz solutions. Do not ask others to give you answers. Work through problems yourself — that's how you learn. First offense = warning, second offense = 1-week suspension, third offense = permanent ban.
  • ©
    Copyright Violation — No Copying Posts — Do not copy, repost, or plagiarize another user's post, question, or content. Every post must be your own original work. 1st violation = 1-week suspension. 2nd violation = permanently banned. No exceptions.
  • 🎯
    Stay On Topic — Use the correct category for your post. Off-topic posts will be removed.
  • 🎓
    Age Requirement — You must be a Middle School (11+), High School, or University student. Users under 13 need parental consent.
  • 🚨
    Report, Don't Retaliate — If you see something wrong, report it. Do not argue or fight back.
  • ⚠
    3-Strike System — Strike 1 = Warning from Edam the Bear. Strike 2 = 1-week suspension (no access to Mathagram). Strike 3 = Permanent account termination. Severe violations (racial slurs, pornography, threats) skip straight to permanent ban.
