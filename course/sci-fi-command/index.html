Sci-Fi Command

The terminal as it appears in science fiction — and the real command-line tools that make your shell look (and work) like a starship console. Hollywood aesthetic, cmatrix, neofetch, htop/btop, nmap, Wireshark, SSH, tmux, Vim, Emacs, ASCII art, LCARS theming, oh-my-zsh, fzf, AI in the terminal — plus deep dives into dotfiles, macOS/Windows terminals, hacker distros (Kali, Parrot, Tails, Qubes), Termux/iSH, JSON/YAML/API tools, database CLIs, Docker/Kubernetes, Git/GitHub CLI, CI/CD, modern Vim/Neovim/Helix, ed/AWK/sed/Bash deep dives, scheduling, reverse engineering (radare2, Ghidra, gdb), cryptography (gpg, age, openssl), privacy (Tor, Tailscale, WireGuard), the hacker library, conferences, and a Hollywood-worthy demo reel capstone. 55 units, 828 lessons.
