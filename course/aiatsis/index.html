AIATSIS — Indigenous Australian Languages

The Australian Institute of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Studies' work on Indigenous languages — the Duolingo-partnership approach to revitalisation. Learn Yolŋu Matha, Pitjantjatjara, Warlpiri, Arrernte, Wiradjuri, Gamilaraay, Noongar, Yawuru, Bundjalung, Kaurna, Torres Strait languages, songlines, kinship systems, and cultural protocols. 25 units, 375 lessons.

Acknowledgement of Country. We acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the lands across Australia and pay our respects to Elders past, present, and emerging. This course is taught with respect for the Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples whose languages, knowledge, and cultures it presents — following AIATSIS Code of Ethics for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Research.
