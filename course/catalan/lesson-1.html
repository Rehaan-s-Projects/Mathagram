The Catalan Alphabet

The Catalan alphabet has 26 letters, just like English. Most letters sound similar, but a few are different:

  • C before e/i sounds like "s" (cel = sky)
  • Ç (c trencada) always sounds like "s" (plaça = square)
  • G before e/i sounds like "zh" (gent = people)
  • J sounds like "zh" in "measure" (jardí = garden)
  • NY sounds like "ñ" in Spanish (any = year)
  • LL sounds like "y" in some dialects (lluna = moon)
  • X sounds like "sh" (xocolata = chocolate)
