Vowel System — Open & Closed

Catalan has seven vowel sounds — more than Spanish! The letters e and o each have two versions: an open version (mouth wide) and a closed version (mouth narrow).

Key Rules

  • The grave accent (è, ò) marks an open vowel.
  • The acute accent (é, ó) marks a closed vowel.
  • À (always grave) is used on stressed a.
  • The open/closed distinction can change meaning: déu (god) vs deu (ten).
