The language of the Na'vi from Pandora in James Cameron's Avatar — created by linguist Paul Frommer. Ejectives, agglutinative infixes, octal numbers, tripartite alignment, tsaheylu, and the songs and clans of Pandora. 20 units, 300 lessons.
