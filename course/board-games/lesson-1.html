"Welcome, young scholar. I am William, and I will teach you to move the checker piece."

Regular pieces can ONLY move forward (never backward!):

  • Y+1 X+1 = forward-right diagonal ↘
  • Y+1 X-1 = forward-left diagonal ↙

Only Kings (promoted pieces) can move backward. Regular pieces go forward only!
