⭐ Capture the Stars — Bronze Challenge

"Welcome, young scholar. I am William, and I will teach you the noble game of Checkers."

The checkers board is 8×8, giving us 64 squares total. But here's the key — only the dark squares are used for play!

Each player starts with 12 pieces, placed on the dark squares of their first 3 rows.

Pieces can only move diagonally forward, one square at a time. Simple to learn, but difficult to master!
