Unit 1 — Checkers (Draughts)

Endgame Techniques

The endgame begins when few pieces remain. Kings become critical, and precise calculation wins.

  • King vs King: One King cannot force a win against another King. It's usually a draw.
  • Two Kings vs One King: Two Kings can force a win by trapping the single King in a corner.
  • The Move: Having "the move" means it's your turn when positions are mirrored — this small advantage can decide the game.
  • Opposition: Keeping your King directly diagonal to the opponent's King controls their movement.
  • Dog Hole: A trapped position in the double corner where a King gets stuck.

Endgame principle: Every move matters. One mistake can turn a win into a draw or a loss.
