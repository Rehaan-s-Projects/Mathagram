Unit 1 — Checkers (Draughts)

Forced Captures Rule

In official checkers rules, if you can make a capture, you must. You cannot choose to make a regular move instead. This is the forced capture rule.

  • If multiple captures are available, you may choose which one to take.
  • Some rule sets require you to take the jump that captures the most pieces (maximum capture rule).
  • The forced capture rule prevents stalling and keeps the game aggressive.
  • If you miss a forced capture, your opponent can remove your piece (the "huff" rule in some versions — mostly outdated).

Tip: You can use forced captures to set traps — sacrifice one piece to force your opponent into a bad position!
