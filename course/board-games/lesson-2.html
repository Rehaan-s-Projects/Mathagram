⭐ Capture the Stars — Silver Challenge

"To capture, young scholar, you jump over your opponent's piece diagonally."

The captured piece is removed from the board entirely.

If after a capture you can capture again, you MUST continue jumping — this is called a multiple jump.

In most rules, captures are forced — if you can capture, you must!
