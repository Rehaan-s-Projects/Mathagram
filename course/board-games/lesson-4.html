⭐ Capture the Stars — Gold Challenge 2

"Now for strategy, young scholar. Control the center of the board."

Keep your pieces together — a lone piece is easy to capture.

The double corner (the corner with two pieces defending each other) is very strong.

Try to advance your pieces together rather than rushing one piece forward.

Trade pieces when you're ahead, avoid trades when you're behind.
