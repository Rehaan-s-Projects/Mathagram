Unit 1 — Checkers (Draughts)

Opening Strategies

The opening is the first few moves of a checkers game. Good openings control the center and prepare for the middle game.

  • Control the center: Pieces in the center have more movement options than pieces on the edges.
  • Advance together: Don't push one piece too far ahead — it can get trapped.
  • Keep your back row: Don't move back-row pieces too early — they block opponent Kings.
  • Named openings: "Old Faithful" (11-15), "Double Corner" (9-14), and "Cross" (11-15, 23-18) are classic openings.

Key principle: Develop your pieces as a team, not individually!
