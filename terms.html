a) Strike 1 — Warning. On your first violation, you will receive a warning message from Edam the Bear. The warning is recorded on your account. Your violating content will be removed. You may continue using Mathagram, but you are expected to follow the rules going forward.

b) Strike 2 — 1-Week Suspension. On your second violation, your account will be suspended for 1 full week (7 days).
